Meeting Highlights

Feb 21 Committee Update

  • A meeting was held with the Alumni Officers. A ₱100 registration fee was confirmed for attendees. Registration proceeds will support school activities managed by the Alumni Officers.
  • Out of 27 Batch 2001 sections, only 8 currently have representatives. Other sections are encouraged to send a representative to the Feb 28 meeting.
  • Pledgers, sponsors, and raffle item partners are now welcome. Please help forward the letter and flyer to potential supporters.
  • Target date: March 17, 2026 — submission of reply slips and committed amounts/items.

Thank you to Rein MJ for the update.

Feb 15 Committee Meeting

  • Fundraising goal set at ₱500,000 to cover event expenses and donate laptop units to the school library.
  • Batch 2001 confirmed as the host batch for the CSNHS 2026 Grand Alumni Homecoming.
  • Host batch contribution set at ₱25,000 per section, with a deadline of March 17, 2026.
